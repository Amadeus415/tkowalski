Current Office Through August 31, 2026



85 Liberty Ship Way, Suite 103
Sausalito CA 94965
We are located in the same building (#85) as Le Garage, the popular French restaurant. PARKING: Park by Building 85; the "parking by permit only" lots are the designated parking for your time at our studio. No parking placard is needed. Our studio is along the waterfront side, facing the serene Sausalito harbor. After you park, head toward the waterfront breezeway lined with palm trees behind Building 85, and you'll see us in Suite #103.



Directions from San Francisco:
  • From 101-N over the Golden Gate Bridge, take Exit 444 for Rodeo Drive.
  • Continue onto Rodeo Drive for 0.2 miles.
  • Turn left on Nevada Street for 0.4 miles.
  • Turn right onto Bridgeway for 0.4 miles.
  • Turn left into Marinship Way, the big left turn that drops down into the harbor area.
  • Turn immediately right onto Liberty Ship Way.
  • Turn left at the big fork to stay on Liberty Ship Way and continue toward Building 85.
  • Destination Building 85 will be on your left.
  • Once parked, walk toward the waterfront breezeway lined with palm trees on the water-facing side of Building 85, and you will see Suite #103.

Directions from North Bay:
  • From 101 South, take Exit 445A toward Marin City / Sausalito.
  • Use the left 2 lanes to turn left onto Donahue Street.
  • Continue onto North Bridge Boulevard.
  • Use the right 2 lanes to turn right onto Bridgeway.
  • Turn left into Marinship Way, the big left turn that drops down into the harbor area.
  • Turn immediately right onto Liberty Ship Way.
  • Turn left at the big fork to stay on Liberty Ship Way and continue toward Building 85.
  • Destination Building 85 will be on your left.
  • Once parked, walk toward the waterfront breezeway lined with palm trees on the water-facing side of Building 85, and you will see Suite #103.



Ferry from San Francisco

The current office is located 1.1 miles from the Sausalito Ferry Terminal, a beautiful 20 minute walk along the waterfront. The Ferry departs from the San Francisco Ferry Building or Fisherman's Wharf and takes 30 minutes. For information on ticket prices and the schedule, please visit here.



Parking

Free parking is available to the left of the 85 Liberty Ship Way building and in the lot behind. The "parking by permit only" lots are the designated parking for your time at our studio. No parking placard is needed.
